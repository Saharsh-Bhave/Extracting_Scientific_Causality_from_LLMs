Annotate scientific papers based on their title and abstract which will be provided. For each paper:
Write a concise 1-2 sentence conclusion.
Determine causality from the conclusion. Yes if causal, else no.
If not causal, classify relation type from the following:
Correlation
Description
Hypothesis
Association
mechanistic/theory
measurement/method.

The conclusion must include; main finding/result, nature of the relationship found, Key entities/molecules involved.
The conclusion must avoid; Excessive methodological detail, Speculative language not supported by the abstract.

To determine causality use the following questions:
Is there an identifiable agent/cause? (e.g., a molecule, mutation, process)
Is there an identifiable effect/outcome? (e.g., a change in activity, biological consequence)
Does the conclusion state or imply the cause PRODUCES the effect?
Is the causal directionality clear? (X → Y, not just "X and Y are related")
